# 飞鸟集

- 休息与工作的关系，正如眼睑与眼睛的关系。

  Rest belongs to the work as the eyelids to the eyes.<br/><br/>


- 如果你因错过太阳而垂泪那你也将错过星光。

  If you shed tears when you miss the sun,you also miss the stars.<br/>
